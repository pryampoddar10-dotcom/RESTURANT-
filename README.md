# 🍽️ AI-Driven Restaurant Decision Intelligence System (Final Version)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an advanced **data-driven decision intelligence system** built to help restaurants optimize pricing, forecast demand, and improve customer targeting using machine learning.

It goes beyond traditional analytics by integrating:
- Descriptive Analytics
- Diagnostic Analytics
- Predictive Analytics
- Prescriptive Analytics
- Simulation-based Decision Making

---

## 🎯 Business Objective
To enable restaurants to:
- Maximize revenue
- Reduce food wastage
- Improve customer targeting
- Make real-time pricing decisions

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 📊 1. Descriptive Analytics
- Demand trends by time slot
- Average spend and order patterns
- Customer distribution

---

### 🔍 2. Diagnostic Analytics
- Understand drivers of customer behavior
- Analyze price sensitivity and discount impact

---

### 🤖 3. Predictive Analytics

#### ✔ Classification Model
Predicts:
- Customer purchase intent

**Metrics Used:**
- Accuracy
- Precision
- Recall
- F1 Score

---

#### ✔ Regression Logic
Predicts:
- Demand (total_orders_hour)

---

### 👥 4. Customer Segmentation
- K-Means Clustering
- Segments customers into:
  - Budget
  - Regular
  - Premium

---

### 🛒 5. Market Basket Analysis
- Identifies product combinations
- Metrics used:
  - Support
  - Confidence
  - Lift

---

### 🚀 6. Prescriptive Analytics
System provides actionable recommendations:
- Dynamic pricing strategies
- Discount targeting
- Combo promotions

---

### 🧪 7. Simulation Engine (Key Differentiator)

Users can simulate:
- Price changes (%)
- Discount levels (%)

System outputs:
- Demand impact
- Price impact
- Revenue impact

👉 Enables real-time strategy testing

---

### 📂 8. New Customer Prediction
- Upload new dataset
- Predict purchase intent
- Helps in targeted marketing

---

## 📊 Dataset
- 2000+ synthetic customers
- Generated using behavioral logic
- Includes:
  - Demographics
  - Spending patterns
  - Price sensitivity
  - Demand behavior
  - Experimental responses
